Sentinel Watch Only

Your online companion for your offline and cold storage bitcoin wallets. Track your offline, cold storage, and paper wallets without needing your private keys.

Sentinel is entirely public key based, meaning your private keys stay private and are never needed or requested.

Keep watch with the Sentinel Watchlist

Add one or many bitcoin wallets to your watchlist to keep an eye on balance and transactions.

The powerful Sentinel watchlist can track all types of bitcoin addresses and extended public keys.

Easily receive into your offline wallets while on the go

Sentinel can derive the next unused address for any wallet in your watchlist.

Generate addresses directly for your offline cold storage or hardware wallet without exposing your private keys or reducing your security.

Build and broadcast transactions using your offline wallet

Use Sentinel to compose and push transactions to the bitcoin network.

Your offline bitcoin wallet may eventually need to send a transaction. Do not compromise the security of your offline wallet by bringing it online. Use Sentinel to maintain the airgap.
